Crop Collect

Crop Collect is a matching game where you must grow and match crops to get points and also earn food. Higher scores will make your pet happier, and possibly more creative and confident. Additionally, by collecting carrots and strawberries, you will be able to feed your pet more healthy snacks!

How to Play

The game begins with a 4x6 grid of growing crops. Each crop has 2-4 stages of growth. Tapping on a crop will automatically mature that crop to its final stage of growth. Additionally, all of the crops above, below, and to the sides of the tapped crop will advance one growth stage if they haven't already reached their final stage. When two or more crops of the same type are next to each other and in their final growth stages, they will be collected and removed from the grid - giving the player points and additional time. All of the crops above them will fall down and take their place, and new crops will come in from the top to fill in the holes. When there are no more moves available or time runs out, the game is over.

Scoring

Collecting 2 of the same crop will get the number of points described above. For more than 2 per crop, a multiplier is applied. For example, 2 cucumbers will give you 100 points, 3 will give you 200 points, 4 will give you 800 points, and so on. Additionally, if after collecting crops, the crops that fell into their place also get collected, those crops will get a bonus as well.

Foot Tennis

Foot Tennis is an aiming game where you must hit targets! Doing well will make your pet happier, healthier, more outgoing, and more friendly!

How to Play

Press and hold on the circle at the bottom of the screen, then drag up and in the direction you want to kick the ball. Let go to throw the ball; the further you drag it from the original circle the farther it will go! Targets in the back are worth more points, as are golden targets, which also give you more balls!

Sweet Cuppin'

Sweet Cuppin' is an observation game where you must pick the right cup to win a prize! Winning can make your pet happier, more organized, and friendlier! You can also score candy and soda!

How to Play

Press the "Start" button and your pet will show you which cup has the prize under it. After that, they will shuffle the cups. After they are done shuffling, you must pick the cup which contains the prize!

Cave Explorer

In Cave Explorer you must guide your pet through a cave, trying to find the staircase to the next floor and get deeper and deeper in the cave. The deeper you get, the better the treasure! But also the more dangerous the obstacles! In addition to finding items like hamburgers and milk, your pet may become more confident and outgoing!

How to Play

Use the directional pad at the bottom of the screen to move your pet in one of four directions. With each step they take, more of the current floor will become visible. Tiles can have different things on them, and walking into or toward those tiles will begin an interaction with them. Some creatures will also follow your pet around and scare them. Once your pet gets too scared, they will leave the cave.
